# Comic Vault Raiders
The application used for cataloging comic books online and always keep the owned titles and wish lists at hand.

The project has two goals.
First, I love reading, collecting, and talking about comics. As my collection grows, it's easier to bring a digital list to Comic Book Fairs to know what to buy and what not to buy.

The second goal is to have a practical project for Java. 
My plan here is to practice backend and some frontend technologies, while also getting to know new trends and tools like OpenAI, Spring AI, WebFlux, etc.
